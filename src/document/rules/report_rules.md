# Report Writing Rules

## Global Rules
- Each section must have at least 5 paragraphs of substantive content
- Each paragraph must contain at least 80 words
- Each section must have a minimum of 600 words total
- Every section must include specific data points, statistics, or measurable metrics
- Every section must include concrete examples or case study references
- Use active voice and varied sentence structure throughout
- Sections must flow logically with clear transition sentences between paragraphs
- Avoid generic filler content — all text must be specific to the topic

## Section-Specific Rules

### Introduction
- Minimum 6 paragraphs
- Must cover: background and context, problem statement, research objectives, scope and delimitations, methodology overview, report organization
- Must include at least 3 specific statistics or data points relevant to the topic
- Must clearly articulate the problem being addressed and its significance
- Must reference at least 2 authoritative sources to establish context
- Minimum 700 words

### Literature Review
- Minimum 6 paragraphs
- Must cover: review of existing work, theoretical foundations, comparative analysis of approaches, gap identification, synthesis of findings, research positioning
- Must cite at least 8 different references from credible sources
- Must identify a clear research gap that the current work addresses
- Must include comparative analysis of at least 3 different approaches or methodologies
- Minimum 800 words

### Methodology
- Minimum 5 paragraphs
- Must cover: research design, data collection methods, experimental setup, evaluation metrics, validation approach
- Must include specific details about tools, technologies, or instruments used
- Must justify methodological choices with reasoning
- Must describe sample size, data sources, or experimental parameters
- Minimum 600 words

### Implementation
- Minimum 5 paragraphs
- Must cover: technology stack overview, implementation details, algorithms and procedures, testing and quality assurance, deployment considerations
- Must include specific technical details such as frameworks, libraries, or platforms used
- Must describe at least 2 concrete implementation challenges and their solutions
- Must include code structure or architectural decisions
- Minimum 600 words

### Results
- Minimum 5 paragraphs
- Must cover: experimental outcomes, quantitative analysis, qualitative observations, comparison with benchmarks, key findings summary
- Must include numerical results with specific values, percentages, or statistical measures
- Must present results in a clear, objective manner
- Must include at least 2 data points comparing against baselines or thresholds
- Minimum 500 words

### Discussion
- Minimum 5 paragraphs
- Must cover: interpretation of results, comparison with prior work, implications and significance, limitations and caveats, future research directions
- Must explain why results are significant and what they mean in a broader context
- Must compare findings with at least 2 prior studies or established benchmarks
- Must honestly address limitations and potential sources of error
- Minimum 600 words

### Conclusion
- Minimum 4 paragraphs
- Must cover: summary of key findings, contributions of the work, limitations encountered, recommendations and future work
- Must synthesize the most important takeaways without introducing new information
- Must explicitly state the contributions and their significance
- Must provide actionable recommendations or clear directions for future research
- Minimum 400 words

### Abstract
- Minimum 3 paragraphs
- Must cover: background context, approach and methodology, key findings and conclusions
- Must be self-contained and understandable without reading the full report
- Must include key numerical results or findings
- Minimum 300 words

### Chapters (Main Body)
- Minimum 5 paragraphs per chapter
- Must have at least 3 subsections per chapter
- Each subsection must have at least 2 paragraphs
- Must include data points, examples, and evidence in each chapter
- Must include transition paragraphs between subsections
- Minimum 600 words per chapter
